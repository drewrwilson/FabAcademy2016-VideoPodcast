Weekly Lectures, in an iTunes-compatible video podcast.

FabAcademy is a 5-month course in digital fabrication. Each week the course has two live-streamed lectures: one from MIT professor Neil Gershenfeld and a guest lecture from a prominent member of the maker movement

Why a podcast? I'm a student in the 2016 class. I work during the day and it's often difficult for me to make it to the live-streamed lecture, so I made this podcast version so I can watch them right after they are published. By subscribing, new lectures are downloaded directly to my phone and I can watch them on my commute home. Hopefully having the videos in this format is useful for you too.
