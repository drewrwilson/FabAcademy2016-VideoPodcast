The Weekly Lectures in an iTunes-Compatible Video Podcast

FabAcademy is a five-month intensive course on digital fabrication, taught by Professor Neil Gershenfeld. This is a video podcast feed of all the video lectures from the 2016 course. There are two lectures each week, one by Prof Gershenfeld and one by a guest lecturer

Subscribe

RSS

Why a podcast? I'm a student in the 2016 class. It's often difficult for me to make it to the live-streamed lecture, because I work during the day. I wrote a script that downloads videos of the streams and publishes them as a podcast. By subscribing videos will be automatically downloaded to your podcast app. All videos are converted to a format that works on most smartphones. This feed is compatible with iTunes so you can manually copy and paste the RSS feed into iTunes. It will be in the iTunes store once it is approved by Apple. Whether or not you're a FabAcademy student, enjoy!
